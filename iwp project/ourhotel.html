Our Story

Marriot was conceptualized in 2003 in VIT, Vellore by 2 university friends: Shaswat Srivastava and Tanya Shree Verma. Like most start-ups, the site’s first iteration was deployed from a garage. In 2006 Shaswat decided to become a professor, so Tanya joined the founding team. Today, our management board consists of CEO Shaswat Srivastava and Tanya Shree Verma
